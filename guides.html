Styling tips

Mix and match like a pro

Build outfits using the 3-2-1 formula: three core staples, two accent pieces, and one wildcard. Experiment with texture, silhouette, and accessories to create infinite combinations.

Classic

Pair a crisp button-down with relaxed denim and add a bold lip for modern polish.

Edgy

Layer a slip dress over a mesh top, add a moto jacket, and finish with lug-sole boots.

Romantic

Mix lace with soft knits, then cinch the waist with a ribbon belt for definition.

Minimalist

Stick to monochrome separates and play with proportions using oversized outerwear.

Bohemian

Blend prints with a shared color family and add handcrafted accessories for texture.

Modernist

Choose architectural silhouettes and anchor them with futuristic accessories.
